PHYSIOTHERAPY EXPERIENCE

My Experiences in Physiotherapy

Life Mark Physiotherapy - Village Square

Overview

Clinical volunteer experience supporting physiotherapists in patient rehabilitation through hands-on assistance with therapeutic modalities and exercise-based treatment.

Key Contributions

  • Assisted in the application and setup of therapeutic modalities including TENS, ultrasound therapy, and shockwave therapy
  • Independently supervised and guided clients during exercises, ensuring proper technique, safety, and adherence to treatment plans
  • Supported patient engagement and comfort during treatment through clear, professional communicationg

Impact & Growth

Developed a strong understanding of clinical practice, including how treatment plans are implemented and adjusted based on patient response. Gained confidence working independently with patients while recognizing the importance of scope of practice and interdisciplinary collaboration. This experience solidified my interest in physiotherapy by providing direct exposure to patient care, clinical reasoning, and the therapeutic relationship.

University of Calgary - Rehabilitation & Fitness Center

Overview

Volunteered in a specialized rehabilitation program for individuals with chronic physical disabilities, supporting participants through individually tailored exercise programs delivered in a supervised group setting.

Key Contributions

  • Assisted participants with strength training, flexibility, and cardiovascular exercises based on individualized rehabilitation plans
  • Supported safe equipment use, exercise execution, and transfers while adapting to varying levels of physical ability
  • Supervised participants during sessions and provided ongoing motivation to enhance engagement and adherence

Impact & Growth

Developed a strong understanding of working with individuals with diverse physical abilities and varying levels of independence. This experience strengthened my ability to adapt exercises and communication styles to meet individual needs, while reinforcing the importance of promoting independence, confidence, and long-term quality of life through rehabilitation.

KNES 479 - Advanced Fitness Appraisal & Exercise Prescription

Overview

Completed KNES 479: Advanced Fitness Appraisal & Exercise Prescription, an applied course focused on assessing physical fitness and designing individualized exercise programs for healthy adults using the CSEP‑PATH framework. Over six months, I worked one‑on‑one with a client to integrate screening, fitness testing, and evidence‑based training principles into a progressive exercise plan.

Key Contributions

  • Conducted comprehensive CSEP health screening and pre‑participation risk stratification to ensure safe exercise participation and guide test selection.
  • Collaboratively set short‑ and long‑term goals with my client, aligning exercise prescription with their fitness level, lifestyle, and readiness for behaviour change.
  • Administered a full musculoskeletal and aerobic fitness battery, including standardized field tests, and accurately recorded results for ongoing comparison.
  • Measured and monitored resting and exercise blood pressure and heart rate to track cardiovascular responses and adjust intensity using CSEP‑based guidelines. ​

Impact & Growth

Strengthened my technical skills in fitness appraisal, data interpretation, and exercise prescription while building confidence communicating results in clear, client‑friendly language. This experience improved my ability to translate CSEP protocols into practical programs that support client safety, motivation, and long‑term adherence to physical activity.
