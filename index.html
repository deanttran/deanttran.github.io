Hi, I'm Dean

I’m a fourth-year Kinesiology student aspiring to become a physiotherapist. My interest in physiotherapy grew from studying human movement and seeing firsthand how impactful the right guidance and rehabilitation can be. To me, physiotherpy isn’t just about exercises and rehabilitation protocols. It’s about helping someone get back to doing what they love. Whether that’s competing, training, or simply moving without pain. It’s about restoring confidence, not just strength. I love the blend of problem-solving, movement analysis, and relationship building that physiotherapy demands. Every injury tells a story, and every recovery is different. That challenge is what excites me most!

This site is a snapshot of where I am now. learning, growing, and working toward becoming the kind of physiotherapist who makes people feel heard, capable, and empowered.
